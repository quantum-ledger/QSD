/* THANKS */

  The QSD public network stands on the shoulders of these open-source
  projects (full list in QSD/source/go.mod):

  - libp2p           — peer-to-peer networking
  - cloudflare/circl — post-quantum signatures (ML-DSA-87 / FIPS 204)
  - wazero          — pure-Go WebAssembly runtime for smart contracts
  - Caddy           — TLS termination + reverse proxy at the edge
  - Prometheus      — operational metrics scrape target
  - Grafana         — dashboard rendering

  And to every operator running a validator, miner, Hive, or Relay against
  the public network — your bug reports, log paste-ins, and "this
  doesn't work" pings are why the public audit score is where it
  is. Thank you.
