The chain's integrity is itself audited. Contract execution semantics are pinned by 4 rows under smart_contracts. See audit.html?category=cryptography for the live view.

Reward penalty watch Tier-3

The validator runs a sliding-window check on every accepted proof's claimed GPU specs. Miners whose mismatch rate crosses the threshold get their per-block share scaled down by the multiplier — the lost share is unminted, never redistributed. Honest miners are bit-identical to the pre-Tier-3 path. See SPEC_ANOMALY_CHECK.md.
